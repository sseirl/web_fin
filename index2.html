CONSTELLATIONS

Orion

Orion is one of the most famous constellations. It is easy to spot with its three bright stars forming the "belt" of the hunter.

Ursa Major

Ursa Major is also known as the Great Bear. Its most famous part is the Big Dipper, which is used to find the North Star.

Scorpius

Scorpius looks like a scorpion. It is best seen in the summer and has the bright red star Antares at its heart.

Cygnus

Cygnus is also called the Northern Cross. It represents a swan flying across the Milky Way.

Leo

Leo is the lion in the sky. Its stars form a shape that looks like a lion lying down.
