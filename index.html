PLANETS OF THE SOLAR SYSTEM

Mercury

The smallest planet and closest to the Sun.

Venus

The hottest planet with a thick atmosphere.

Earth

Our home planet, the only known one to support life.

Mars

Known as the Red Planet due to iron oxide on its surface.

Jupiter

The largest planet, famous for its Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Known for its stunning ring system.

Uranus

A blue-green planet that rotates on its side.

Neptune

The farthest planet, known for its deep blue color.
